Poetry

For the things which cant be remembered| You are those who appear when time and circumstance cross| Thus recreating that memory which was lost|
This thing which I see is not beneath me| This thing is above me; This thing is all around me| Even in color, even in sound| That which I write, even contains a tiny piece of light| This light travels from from me and you| Where does it go?|
Happy is when I am me| When Im happy I cant see| Happiness or sadness its hard to see those right beside me|
Love to you is for granted| Love to you is a will to live| Love to you resurrects quickly|
The trees talk to each other| As it stands, we should too| A burning passion fuels the heart| Existence fuels the soul|
The moon glimmers and winks to the blind| Imagine your now (reality darkens)| Normality is not conformality|
Conformality is normality| Normality is imaginary| The sun shines to the close minded|
Pages are from trees| Memories are sowed in the heart| We forget, just as nature does| Nature doesnt forget but I do| Forgiveness is pain maybe| At least we got to start again|
